Hi there! I'm Minkyong. I'm an aspiring UX designer, researcher, and a graduate student currently earning my Master's degree in Human-Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE) at the University of Washington.

I get extremely passionate about solving problems within constraints using both research and data. I strongly believe in creating meaningful online experiences for people in various contexts.

Prior to UW, I was an undergraduate student at Northwestern University, studying Communication Studies, Cognitive Science, and Integrated Marketing Communications.

While there, I wrote on honors thesis on self-presentational concerns people hold when disclosing their emotions on Facebook, which can be found here.

When not immersed in design, I can be found hunting for caffeine, travelling, or enjoying the outdoors.

Research Interests

Based on my prior experience with academic research, I am broadly interested in computer-mediated communication, helping users better engage with technologies that promote social interaction.
